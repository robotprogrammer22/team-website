Sponsors

Team 60 has built great relationships with many local businesses. Our community, though small, has a generous heart and supportive nature. Each year, donations of time and money enable our continued existence. The majority of our funding is tax credit donations from local businesses and individuals. Several companies also donate time, equipment, and other resources to our team.

We proudly share the logo of each business sponsoring us. By putting their name on our robot, we show our gratitude and provide value in advertising to our sponsors. And after high school, some students become employees of these companies through apprenticeship programs. This benefits everyone.

Our thanks to those who supported us this year!
