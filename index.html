FRC Team 60 - Bionic Bulldogs

Team 60 is a competitive robotics team based in Kingman Arizona. We compete in the FIRST Robotics Competition(FRC). Each year since 1997 we've designed, built, and programmed a new robot. Our members are high school students from Kingman High School, Kingman Academy of Learning (KAOL), Lee Williams High School, homeschool, and K-12 Virtual Academy.

Our Goal: Promote STEM in our community by supporting each FIRST program in our schools. These are FIRST Robotics Competition, FIRST Tech Challenge, FIRST Lego League, and Lego League Jr.

What is FIRST?

For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology, FIRST is a portal for K-12 students allowing exploration of the STEM world. It helps us find what we like to do. Each year there is a new game and new robots to build. But FIRST is more than robots. It is about becoming a better person and learning valuable life skills such as teamwork, Gracious Professionalism and Coopertition. FIRST is where ingenuity and intellect meet to create the hardest fun ever.

What is FRC?

FIRST Robotics Competition is the program for high school students. With strict rules, limited resources, and an intense schedule, we create and program industrial sized robots for each years game. The robot is bigger, playing field larger, and the competition fierce.
